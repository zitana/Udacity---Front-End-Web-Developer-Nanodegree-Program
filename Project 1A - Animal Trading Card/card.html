Giant pouched rat

Interesting fact: The rats have been used to to detect land mines in Africa.

  • Scientific name: Cricetomys
  • Avarage size: Item #2
  • Avarage weight: 0,5-1,5 kg
  • Habitat: sub-Saharan Africa

Giant pouched rats are only distantly related to the true rats. They are omnivorous and feed on vegetation and small animals, especially insects. They have a particular taste for palm nuts. They are nocturnal animals.
